About Sweet Crumbs Bakery

Sweet Crumbs Bakery is a local bakery focused on fresh, handcrafted baked goods made daily. We specialise in cakes, cupcakes, pastries, and artisan breads made with quality ingredients.

Our goal is simple: create delicious baked goods that feel personal, affordable, and memorable. Every product is made with care, consistency, and attention to detail.

We also create custom cakes for birthdays, weddings, and special events tailored exactly to your vision.
