About Sweet Crumbs Bakery

Sweet Crumbs Bakery is a small, local bakery focused on creating fresh, high-quality baked goods every day. From cakes and cupcakes to breads and pastries, everything is made daily using simple ingredients and careful preparation.

The bakery was built on one idea: provide delicious, well-made treats at prices people can afford. Alongside everyday favorites, we also create custom cakes designed to match special occasions like birthdays, celebrations, and events.

We aim to create a welcoming experience for every customer. Whether you're stopping by for fresh bread or ordering something custom, you can expect friendly service and consistent quality every time.

Our Goals

  • Increase awareness of Sweet Crumbs Bakery
  • Showcase our range of fresh products
  • Make it easy to request custom orders
  • Provide clear contact and location information
  • Grow customer enquiries and overall sales
