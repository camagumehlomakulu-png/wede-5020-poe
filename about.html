About Sweet Crumbs Bakery

Sweet Crumbs Bakery is a small local bakery focused on creating fresh, high-quality baked goods every single day. From cakes and cupcakes to breads and pastries, every item is prepared carefully using quality ingredients and homemade baking methods.

The bakery was built on one simple idea: to provide delicious baked treats at affordable prices while creating memorable experiences for customers.

Alongside our everyday favourites, we also specialise in custom-made cakes designed for birthdays, weddings, celebrations, and special events.
